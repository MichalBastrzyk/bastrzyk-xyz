# What am I using?

This is a list of all the tools and services I use on a daily basis.

## Hardware

- **Iphone 15** 128GB
- 14 inch 2021 **MacBook Pro M1 Pro** w/ 16GB RAM and 512GB SSD
- 1x **32 inch 4K Monitor** - ACER NITRO XV2 XV322QKKV
- **Logitech MX Master 3s** - Mouse
- Custom GMK67 65% Wireless Mechanical Keyboard
- Ajazz Palm Rest
- **Sony WH-CH720N** -  Wireless Noise Cancelling Headphones
- DreamMachines large mouse pad

## Software

- [Google Chrome](https://www.google.com/chrome/) - The browser (might be replaced with [Brave](https://brave.com/) in the future)
- [Notion](https://www.notion.so/) - Organization and some Notes
- Apple Notes - My go to for writing simple notes and some documents that need collaboration
- Apple Mail - My go to for email
- Apple Calendar - My go to for scheduling
- [Spotify](https://www.spotify.com/) - Music

### Tools

- [Zed](https://zed.dev/) - A better editor
- [Ghostty](https://ghostty.org) - A better terminal
- [My dotfiles](https://github.com/MichalBastrzyk/dotfiles) - My dotfiles that use `stow` as the manager and `brew`
- [Orbstack](https://orbstack.dev/) - Faster containers and VMs on Mac (might be replaced with [Container](https://github.com/apple/container) from apple in the future)
- [AstroJS](https://astro.build) - My framework of choice for buildling websites, ecommerce and blogs
- [React](https://reactjs.org/) - My framework of choice for building UIs
- [TailwindCSS](https://tailwindcss.com/) - My framework of choice for styling
- [shadcn/ui](https://github.com/shadcn/ui) - My components library of choice

### Services

- [Perplexity](https://www.perplexity.ai/) - My recent find **very, very good** for learning and creating new stuff.
- [t3.chat](https://t3.chat/) - My favourite LLM chat app
- [Vercel](https://vercel.com/) and [Cloudflare](https://cloudflare.com/) - My serverless hosting and CDN
- [Hetzner](https://hetzner.com/) - My VPS provider
- [Mullvad VPN](https://mullvad.net/) - My VPN provider (if I need one)
- [Roamless](https://roamless.com/) - My global ESIM provider (very useful for travelling)
